The Lord Rama

Personal Summery

Lord Rama, a revered hero whose story echoes through the ages. With unwavering dedication to truth and justice, Stand as a symbol of courage and righteousness. Endowed with divine strength and guided by a steadfast moral compass, Inspire others to embrace their inner heroes.My oble character, unwavering leadership, and formidable combat prowess make me a beloved figure, embodying the essence of goodness and valor

Date Of birth

January 10 at 12.05 hours, 5114 BC

Birth Place

Ayodhya (Uttar Pradesh, India)

In family of Dasharatha(a great king of Ayodhya)

Seventh Avatar of Lord Vishnu

Skill and Ability

  • Supreme archer, unmatched precision, wields divine bow "Sharanga."
  • Strategic leader, wise decision-maker, inspires and guides allies.
  • Master warrior, immense strength, unmatched combat skills, performs valorous feats.
  • Unwavering righteousness, inspires moral values, embodiment of justice and integrity.
  • Mighty strength, performs extraordinary feats, overpowering adversaries with ease.

Awards and Experiance

  • Childhood education, marriage to Sita, fourteen-year exile with Sita and Lakshmana as per father's decree.
  • During exile, encountered Surpanakha; formed strong bond with Sugriva and Hanuman.
  • Architect of the majestic Rama Setu, bridging lands and legends.
  • Lord Rama defeated Ravana with the help of Hanuman's army and returned as the rightful king of Ayodhya.
  • Lord Rama's reign: fair, prosperous, harmonious, bringing peace to Ayodhya.
